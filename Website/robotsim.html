Differential Drive Robot using ROS2.

In the ongoing pursuit of learning Robotics Operating System(ROS2) this is a simple project demonstarating some key concepts of ROS2 such as Nodes, Topics, Services, Messages, Parameters, Actions, URDF, Transforms(tf), and Gazebo simulator basics.

The whole process from installing a dual-boot linux system and installing required ROS2 packages to debugging not just the code I wrote, but figuring out why certain aspects of ROS2 or Gazebo weren't working due to missing dependencies or mismatched/incompatible versions of some librarys was enlighing. Limited troubleshooting resources due to the uniqueness of problems I was having left me neck deep in debugging code written by various developers and trying to reach out to them to figure out what some of the code meant. In the end, having setup the environment for the applications to work reliably, I moved on to doing simple projects and continue to learn ROS2 while also documenting the problems I face and maintain debugging notes.
