Vehicle Dynamics Engineer at Team Manipal Racing

Following a rigorous task-phase I was selected as one of the three people out of about 30 to be a Vehicle Dynamics Engineer for Team Manipal Racing. I collaborated with a cross-functional team of 35 students to design a BAJA-SAE vehicle. I played a key role in the designing, fabrication, assembly, and testing of critical vehicle components, including steering knuckles and suspension linkages. Utilizing hand calculations and iterative design optimization techniques, I successfully developed steering knuckles that were 20% lighter, enhancing overall vehicle performance. This hands-on experience allowed me to demonstrate proficiency in automotive engineering, manufacturing processes and the use of machine tools, contributing significantly to our team's success in competitions.

Skills:
Solidworks - 3D CAD, Mechanical analysis
Catia V5 - 3D CAD
Wingeo - Suspension geometry design
Excel - Engineering calculations
Manufacturing - Lathe, CNC mill, Waterjet, Welding, etc.
