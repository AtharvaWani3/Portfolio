Flow and Mixing Analysis of Chemical Mixers

As a Mechanical Engineer Intern - R&D at ACME Process Systems I spearheaded the integration of Ansys Fluent into the design process of chemical mixers. As the company transitioned from traditional design methodologies to a more research focussed development model to innovate impeller designs for chemical mixing, I played a crucial role in the infant stages of the new R&D department.

After learning the traditional methods, I proposed the use of Ansys-Fluent for Computational Fluid Dynamic analysis of mixing and flow of the contents of industrial mixing tanks. I translated some commonly used and few novel impeller and mixing tank designs from AutoCAD manufacturing drawings to 3D CAD models optimized for fluid flow analysis to setup a procedure and gather baseline data.

After completing the analysis for each model, I documented the Mixing properties and Power Efficiencies. Towards the end of the internship I presented a well structured procedure to create new designs and analyze them using Ansys-Fluent.

Ansys-Fluent - Computational Fluid Dynamics
