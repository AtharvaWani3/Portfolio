Home-use Neurological Disability Assistive Technology Device

I completed this project under the guidance of Dr. Troy McDaniel in collaboration with Barrow Neurological Institute, Phoenix, AZ.

This project explores the critical safety and ethical challenges posed by the increasing adoption of autonomous vehicles. The research identifies and delves into an emergency, high-risk and ethically challenging scenario that often results in severe accidents, despite the technological advancements by industry leaders like Tesla and Waymo. By analyzing real-world data and simulations, the project proposes innovative methods to minimize accident severity, contributing to the ongoing discourse on enhancing the safety and reliability of autonomous driving technology. This work is pivotal in ensuring that autonomous vehicles can navigate complex and ethically challenging situations safely, fostering greater trust and wider adoption of this transformative technology.
